# ClipSync Quick Start Guide

## Get Started in 5 Minutes

ClipSync is designed to get you up and running quickly. Follow these simple steps to start syncing your clipboard across all your devices.

---

## Step 1: Sign Up (30 seconds)

1. Go to [clipsync.com](https://clipsync.com)
2. Click **"Get Started Free"**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Or sign up with Google/GitHub for faster setup

**Tip:** Use the same email on all devices for seamless sync.

---

## Step 2: Download the App (1 minute)

Choose your platform:

### Desktop (Recommended)
- **Windows**: Download `.exe` installer or portable version
- **Mac**: Download `.dmg` file
- **Linux**: Download `.AppImage` or `.deb` file

### Mobile
- **iOS**: Download from App Store
- **Android**: Download from Google Play

### Browser
- **Chrome**: Install from Chrome Web Store
- **Firefox**: Install from Firefox Add-ons

**Pro Tip:** Install on all your devices for the full experience.

---

## Step 3: Sign In and Grant Permissions (30 seconds)

1. Open ClipSync on your device
2. Sign in with your account
3. Grant clipboard permissions when prompted
4. That's it! ClipSync is now running

**What happens:**
- ClipSync starts monitoring your clipboard
- Your first clip is captured automatically
- Sync begins immediately (if multiple devices)

---

## Step 4: Start Copying (10 seconds)

Just start copying as normal! ClipSync captures everything:

**Try copying:**
- A code snippet from your IDE
- A URL from your browser
- Some text from a document
- An image from anywhere

**View your clips:**
- Desktop: Press `Ctrl+Shift+H` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+H` (Mac)
- Mobile: Open the ClipSync app
- Browser: Click the extension icon

---

## Step 5: Sync Across Devices (Optional)

If you have ClipSync on multiple devices:

1. Sign in with the same account on all devices
2. Copy something on Device A
3. Switch to Device B
4. Press `Ctrl+Shift+V` (or open the app)
5. Paste the content â€“ it's there!

**That's the magic of real-time sync.**

## First Things to Try

### 1. Copy on Desktop, Paste on Phone
1. Copy a code snippet on your computer
2. Open ClipSync on your phone
3. Paste the snippet

**Result:** Instant sync, no manual transfer needed.

### 2. Transform Text
1. Copy some text (e.g., "myVariableName")
2. Open ClipSync
3. Click the transform icon
4. Select "snake_case"

**Result:** Text is converted to "my_variable_name"

### 3. Create a Snippet
1. Copy something you use frequently
2. Right-click and select "Save as Snippet"
3. Give it a name
4. Access anytime from the snippet library

**Result:** Quick access to frequently used content.

### 4. Share with Team (Team Plan)
1. Create a team space
2. Invite team members
3. Copy something to the shared space
4. Team members see it instantly

**Result:** Seamless team collaboration.

## Setting Up Workspaces

Organize your clips by project or context:

1. Click the **Spaces** icon (or press `Ctrl+Shift+1`)
2. Click **"Create New Space"**
3. Name it (e.g., "Project Alpha", "Personal", "Work")
4. Choose a color
5. Start copying in that space

**Switch spaces:**
- Keyboard: `Ctrl+Shift+1`, `Ctrl+Shift+2`, etc.
- UI: Click the space name in the header

## Troubleshooting

### "Clipboard isn't syncing"

**Check:**
1. Are you signed in on both devices?
2. Is your internet connection stable?
3. Is sync enabled in settings?

**Try:**
- Refresh sync: Settings â†’ Sync â†’ Force Refresh
- Restart the app
- Check sync status in settings

### "Clips aren't being captured"

**Check:**
1. Did you grant clipboard permissions?
2. Is ClipSync running in the background?
3. Is clipboard monitoring enabled?

**Try:**
- Restart ClipSync
- Re-grant permissions
- Check settings for monitoring status

### "App is slow"

**Check:**
1. How many clips do you have? (10,000+ can slow things down)
2. Are you on an older computer?
3. Is your disk nearly full?

**Try:**
- Clear old clips (Settings â†’ Clear History)
- Enable auto-cleanup (Settings â†’ Auto-delete after 30 days)
- Close other apps to free memory

## Tips for Maximum Productivity

**1. Use Keyboard Shortcuts**
Don't click when you can press a key. Memorize the shortcuts â€“ they'll save you hours.

## Security Best Practices

**1. Enable Per-Clip Encryption**
For sensitive clips (passwords, API keys), enable password protection.

**2. Use Strong Passwords**
Your account password protects your encryption keys. Make it strong.

**3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
Add an extra layer of security to your account.

**4. Review Connected Devices**
Regularly check which devices have access to your account.

**5. Clear Sensitive Clips**
After using sensitive content, clear it from your clipboard and history.
