## Best Practices for Email Campaign

### Timing
- **Launch Email**: Send at 10 AM EST on Tuesday
- **Follow-ups**: Space 2-3 days apart
- **Win-back**: Send after 14+ days of inactivity

### Segmentation
- **Free users**: Focus on upgrade benefits
- **Trial users**: Feature education and value reinforcement
- **Pro users**: Advanced features and community building
- **Team users**: Collaboration features and admin tools
- **Inactive users**: Win-back campaigns with special offers

### Personalization
- Use recipient's name
- Reference their plan (Free/Pro/Team)
- Mention features they've used
- Suggest relevant features based on usage

### A/B Testing
- Test subject lines
- Test CTA button text
- Test email length
- Test send times
- Test offer variations

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 25-35%)
- Click-through rate (target: 3-5%)
- Conversion rate (target: 1-3%)
- Unsubscribe rate (target: <0.5%)
- Revenue per email

### Compliance
- Include physical address
- Provide unsubscribe link
- Comply with CAN-SPAM and GDPR
- Honor opt-outs promptly
