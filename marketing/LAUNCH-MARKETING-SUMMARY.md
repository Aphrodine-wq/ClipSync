## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks
1. **Technical Issues:** Server crashes, bugs on launch day
   - Mitigation: Load testing, backup servers, rapid response team

2. **Low Engagement:** Poor Product Hunt performance
   - Mitigation: Pre-launch audience building, multiple launch attempts

3. **Negative Reviews:** Critical feedback on launch day
   - Mitigation: Beta testing, address known issues, responsive support

4. **Competitor Response:** Competitors release similar features
   - Mitigation: Emphasize unique features, focus on developer experience

5. **Pricing Resistance**: Users find pricing too high
   - Mitigation: Strong value proposition, launch discounts, free tier
